School Principal

Elias Muniz

It is a privilege and honor to be the principal and educational leader of our Mag­nificent Monarch Community. We are proud to be recently named a 2014 California Distinguished School. Our namesake, Phoebe Apperson Hearst, was a staunch supporter of education, service to the community, the early kindergarten movement and co-founded the Parent/Teacher Association (PTA). When you step foot on our campus you will immediately feel our strong commitment towards children and the community in which they live. We are a community of learners in a positive and safe school culture.
